Web design & Hosting

Web Design and e-commerce solutions.

The majority of websites we create use WordPress (like this one), WordPress is a content
management system (CMS). This CMS gives you the ability to keep your website up to date,
don’t worry if you’re not sure how to do that. It’s our job at Tec Team to help, we can do that by
providing training for your company or simply do changes for you. For online shops (e-
commerce) we prefer to work as a Shopify Partner providing the same design and support to you.
Shopify is a e-commerce platform that is fast, secure and most importantly scalable to the sales
you aspire to.

Hosting and email

Without web hosting a website cannot exist, its online storage for the pages of your website.
Our hosting fee’s are very competitive. With web hosting comes basic email support with limited
email storage, we always recommend using a dedicated business email service. At Tec Team we
are a Google Workplace reseller which not only provides all your email storage needs but also much
more like, cloud storage, calendar’s, contact lists etc.
